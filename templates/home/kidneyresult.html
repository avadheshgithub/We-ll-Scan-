Tests Recommended

Serum Creatinine Test

Measures the level of creatinine
in the blood, which indicates how
well the kidneys are filtering
waste products from the blood.

Urinalysis

Examines a urine sample for
abnormalities such as protein, blood,
or infection, which can provide insights
into kidney function and health.

Glomerular Filtration Rate

Estimates the rate at which the
kidneys filter waste from the
blood, provide a more accurate
assessment of kidney function.

Electrolyte Panel

Measures levels of potassium,
sodium, calcium, and
phosphorus in blood, as imbalances
can occur in kidney disease

Albumin-Creatinine Ratio

Calculates the ratio of albumin
to creatinine in the urine,
helping to detect early
signs of kidney damage.

Blood Pressure Monitoring

Regular monitoring of blood
pressure is essential, as high
blood pressure can both contribute
to result in kidney disease.
